What is Four Toppings?

Four Toppings is a top rated bubble tea shop around the world. We have a Michellin Star and multiple nominations for "Best Tea Shop" from newspapers like the New York Times. We strive to use the highest quality ingredients and to provide the best customer service to our beloved fans.

How can I help?

You can help us run Four Toppings all by yourself! You take orders from our customers, pour teas and add toppings accordingly, and serve! Not to mention, you also have to keep track of the supplies you have and make sure you don't run out, or else we'll get an angry review :(

Make sure to make an account to save your progress, and we can help you figure out what to do along the way!
